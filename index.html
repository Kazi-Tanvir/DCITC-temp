DATA SECURITY

Encryption

Do you want to protect your information in the digital world? Encryption is a process of transforming information by which the information is made unreadable to unauthorized users. That is, any digital content, such as text or images, is converted into a special language so that no one other than the authorized person can read or understand the information.

256-bit Standard Key Size
99.9% Protection Rate

Bybit Crypto Heist

By stealing approximately $1.5 billion Ethereum (ETH) and stETH, equivalent to $3.67 trillion USD, the Bybit hack on February 21, 2025, became the biggest cyber theft ever. The Lazarus Group carried out the attack by injecting malicious JavaScript to bypass security measures.

$3.67T Stolen Value
2025 Attack Year
INFORMATION SECURITY

Steganography

Steganography is the technique of hiding information within images, audio or text, making it nearly impossible to detect. Unlike encryption, which scrambles data, it keeps messages hidden in plain sight for secure communication. Used in privacy, security, and creative fields, it's a fascinating way to conceal information. The question is—can you find what's hidden?

440 BC First Known Use
10% Payload Capacity

AI Chip Revolution

Ever thought of processing data without electricity? Researchers at the University of Pennsylvania have developed an AI chip (150 nm) smaller than a grain of salt that uses light to process data. This photonic chip enables faster, more efficient AI computations with minimal energy consumption.

Bybit Crypto Heist

By stealing approximately $1.5 billion Ethereum (ETH) and stETH, equivalent to $3.67 trillion USD, the Bybit hack on February 21, 2025, became the biggest cyber theft ever. The Lazarus Group carried out the attack by injecting malicious JavaScript to bypass security measures.

SPACE SCIENCE

Gravitational Waves

Gravitational waves are ripples in spacetime caused by the acceleration of massive objects. They provide insights into cosmic events, test General Relativity, and open new avenues for studying the universe. The first direct detection occurred on September 14, 2015, by LIGO.

2015 First Detection
1.3B Light-years Away
HEALTH SCIENCE

HMPV Virus

Human metapneumovirus (HMPV) is an RNA virus belonging to the Pneumoviridae family. According to IEDCR, Bangladesh has been encountering this virus since 2017. The virus causes upper respiratory infections and has a very low mortality rate, but threatens those with asthma or COPD.

2017 First Detected
<1% Mortality Rate

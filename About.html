About Us

Dhaka College IT Club (DCITC) is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering technological innovation and digital literacy among students. Founded in 2018, we've grown into a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, programmers, designers, and future digital leaders.

Our Vision

To create a hub of technological innovation where students can explore, learn, and grow into future tech leaders of Bangladesh, prepared to meet the challenges of an increasingly digital world.

Our Mission

We aim to foster technical excellence, encourage innovation, and build a collaborative community where students can develop practical IT skills through workshops, projects, and mentorship.

What We Do

We organize workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, tech talks, and project collaborations to enhance practical skills and prepare students for industry challenges.

About Us

Dhaka College IT Club (DCITC) is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering technological innovation and digital literacy among students. Founded in 2018, we've grown into a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, programmers, designers, and future digital leaders.

Our Vision

To create a hub of technological innovation where students can explore, learn, and grow into future tech leaders of Bangladesh, prepared to meet the challenges of an increasingly digital world.

Our Mission

We aim to foster technical excellence, encourage innovation, and build a collaborative community where students can develop practical IT skills through workshops, projects, and mentorship.

What We Do

We organize workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, tech talks, and project collaborations to enhance practical skills and prepare students for industry challenges.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member of DCITC?

To become a member, you need to be a current student of Dhaka College. We open applications at the beginning of each semester. Fill out the membership form online and attend an orientation session. No prior technical knowledge is required!

What activities does the club organize?

We organize a variety of activities including weekly workshops, monthly hackathons, coding competitions, tech talks by industry professionals, project collaborations, and annual tech festivals. We also provide mentorship programs for members interested in specific areas of technology.

Do I need prior programming experience to join?

Not at all! We welcome members of all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced programmers. We have dedicated tracks and mentorship programs for beginners to help you start your tech journey.

Are there any membership fees?

Yes, there is a nominal annual membership fee of 500 BDT which covers your access to all regular workshops, events, and the club's resources. However, we offer fee waivers for students who may not be able to afford it.

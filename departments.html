Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our departments and services.

How do departments collaborate on projects?

Our departments work seamlessly together through an integrated workflow system. We use agile methodologies with regular cross-functional meetings to ensure all aspects of a project are aligned. Each project gets assigned a dedicated project manager who coordinates between departments, ensuring efficient communication and timely delivery.

What technologies do your departments specialize in?

Our Web Development team specializes in React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, and PHP. The UI/UX Design team is proficient with Figma, Adobe XD, and Sketch. Our AI & Machine Learning department works with TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Python. The Digital Marketing team utilizes various SEO tools, analytics platforms, and social media management solutions to deliver results-driven campaigns.

How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard website might take 4-8 weeks, while a comprehensive e-commerce platform could take 3-6 months. AI solutions typically require 2-4 months of development and training. We provide detailed timelines during the project planning phase and keep you updated on progress throughout the development lifecycle.

Do you offer maintenance and support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various maintenance and support packages tailored to your needs. These include regular updates, security patches, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. Our support team is available during business hours, with options for 24/7 support for mission-critical applications.

How do you ensure the quality of your deliverables?

Quality assurance is integrated into every step of our process. We implement rigorous testing methodologies including unit testing, integration testing, and user acceptance testing. Our QA team works independently from the development teams to ensure unbiased evaluation. We also follow industry best practices and standards specific to each department's area of expertise.
